90-Day Implementation Roadmap

A proven framework for rapid AI adoption with minimal risk and maximum business impact:

1

Pilot Selection

Week 1
  • Choose 1-2 highest priority use cases
  • Identify pilot team (5-10 users)
  • Set measurable success criteria
  • Secure necessary resources and budget
Deliverable: Approved pilot scope document
2

Rapid Prototyping

Weeks 2-4
  • Build minimum viable product (MVP)
  • Test with pilot users daily
  • Gather feedback and iterate quickly
  • Refine based on real usage patterns
Deliverable: Working prototype with user feedback
3

Scaled Deployment

Weeks 5-8
  • Deploy to broader team (20-50 users)
  • Provide comprehensive training
  • Monitor adoption and usage metrics
  • Measure ROI and business impact
Deliverable: Performance metrics and ROI report
4

Optimization & Expansion

Weeks 9-12
  • Optimize based on usage data
  • Plan additional use cases
  • Scale successful pilots company-wide
  • Develop long-term AI strategy
Deliverable: Expansion roadmap and strategy
